Why Us?

We are a group of young and motivated students, who are inspired to bring about a positive change by bridging the gap between humans and machines.


The lack of quality knowledge and free resources, drove us to come up with an innovative solution to make an all in one platform to spread Covid-19 awareness, promote vaccination and help doctors with an A.I Detection Model.
